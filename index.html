About me

My name is Marelius Moen (if you wonder how to pronounce it check out the audio-file above), I’m a 32 your old guy from a city in northern Norway called Tromsø, but now I live in Varberg, Sweden. Tromsø is cold, usually snowing 9 months and that is the reason why i started to play basketball, an indoor sport; when i was 11 years old. I played until I was 18 and by that time I became a rookie at Tromsø Storm the 2nd best team in the top league in Norway. This experience thought me about hard work and persistence, if you work hard enough you will reach your goal in the end, thats a lesson I have remembered and implemented in my career and it has taken me all over the world. ​ Now after more than a decade working in high-end restaurants and hotels I felt the need to change pace and direction and took the leap and changed carreer path. I started a Diploma in Software Development at Code Institute, and the rest is history. I love the problem solving aspect of the job and to create something that is both logical and creative. ​ Would you like to see more of what I’m up to on my own time check out my instagram link below.
